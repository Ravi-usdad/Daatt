Setting Prices

How to Set Your Price for Resources

Congratulations on becoming an uploader on Daatt! This guide will help you set the right price for your resources so that you can make the most money possible while also providing value to our users.

Step 1

Determine the Value of Your Resource

The first step in setting your price is to determine the value of your resource. Consider factors like the amount of time and effort you put into creating it, the quality of the content, and its uniqueness. You should also research similar resources on our platform to see what they are priced at.

Step 2

Consider Your Target Audience

Your target audience can also impact the price of your resource. Think about the age range, grade level, and subject matter of your intended audience. Resources for higher grade levels or specialized subjects may be priced higher than resources for younger students or more general topics..

Step 3

Set Your Price

Based on your research and analysis, you can now set your price. Remember that as an uploader, you will receive a percentage of the sale price depending on your membership tier. Keep this in mind when setting your price to ensure you are making the profit you desire. Additionally, consider offering occasional discounts to encourage more sales.

You have the freedom to set your own price for each resource you upload. Simply enter the price you want to charge in the designated field on the upload page, and we'll automatically set it to its credit value.

Here's an example: Let's say you upload a resource that you believe is worth $10.00 USD. Set the price at $10.00 (which would earn you $7.00 USD based on our 30% commission for free members). On the website, it will automatically convert to credits and tell users that the price is 20 credits.

Whichever pricing option you choose, remember that the value of your resource should reflect the time, effort, and expertise you put into creating it. Don't undervalue your work, but also keep in mind that setting too high of a price may deter potential buyers.

Step 4

Keep an Eye on Your Resource

It's important to monitor how your resource is performing and adjust your price as necessary. If you are not receiving many purchases, consider lowering your price or offering discounts. Conversely, if your resource is very popular, you may be able to raise the price.

We hope this guide helps you set the perfect price for your resources on Daatt. If you have any additional questions or concerns, please feel free to contact us. Happy uploading!
